The World

NeoVarion

A world suspended between ancient magic and cosmic terror. At its heart lies the kingdom of Slinsil, built unknowingly above the planet's core. To destroy Slinsil is to destroy everything.

The Arcwatch

A device of alien origin, fused to the wrist of Max Strange by the dying alien Magnulos. It grants the ability to transform into powerful alien forms, each with a unique power. It was once used to defeat Vael, Krill's father. Now it is the only weapon that can stop his son.

Magnulos

The last survivor of a destroyed planet, alongside Krill. He spent his final strength flying across the universe to reach NeoVarion and warn its people. He found Max on the streets of Slinsil. Before dying from his wounds, he passed the Arcwatch and everything that mattered to a street kid with nothing to lose.

The Shadowfang

A rare serpent whose bite does not kill the body. It corrupts the heart slowly, feeding on ambition and turning it into obsession. It is the reason two brothers became arch enemies. It is the reason the Forest of Haag fell into darkness.

The Dwellers of Haag

Small goblin-like beings covered in thick brown fur with large reflective eyes. They share a deep spiritual bond with the Forest of Haag, feeling every tree as if it were their own heartbeat. Peaceful by nature, but fiercely protective of their home when threatened.

The Spies

Five Unlikely Heroes

01
Leader, Arcwatch Bearer
Max Strange
An orphan street kid from Slinsil with no family, no home, and no future, until the night Magnulos crashed on NeoVarion and chose him above all others. He carries the Arcwatch, the weight of a dying alien's last hope, and the lives of everyone around him.
02
Wizard
Touchfire
A wizard who grew up dreaming of greatness alongside his brother. That brother became his greatest enemy after a Shadowfang snake changed everything. Touchfire walks the same path his brother chose, but with a different heart. By the end, he carries both his own power and his brother's, and the grief of what that means.
03
Warrior
Sarason
Daughter of Slinsil's greatest general. Rejected by the army for acting on impulse instead of thought. Cold and focused on the surface, she softens slowly as the mission forces her to trust others. She nearly dies alone on a riverbank protecting the battle plans. She earns her father's rank in the end.
04
Elf, Scout
Calypso
From Veyln, a hidden elven city built underground and lit by glowing crystals. Exiled by her own people for reasons she rarely speaks about. She joined the mission because the king of Slinsil offered her something her people never did: a place to belong. Observant and wise, she speaks little but means everything when she does.
05
Dwarf
Littlejohn
Kicked out of Clan Durnhold for being too weak by dwarven standards. He joined purely for gold and told himself that was enough. Somewhere between five spider bites, a near-fatal river crossing, and waking up to find his friends still waiting, he stopped pretending. His complaints hide a dwarf terrified of not being enough.

The Enemy

Krill

He did not earn his power. He inherited it. His father Vael spent centuries destroying civilizations across multiple galaxies, absorbing their knowledge, growing stronger with each world he erased. When Magnulos defeated Vael, he spared his life. That was the last mistake anyone made.

Krill carries everything his father built and one purpose of his own: finish what Vael started, and destroy the one who stopped him. Magnulos is dead. But the watch he left behind is not.

Every world he destroys, he changes. By the time he reaches Slinsil, he is barely recognizable. He is something between a king and a god.

The Dark Wizard

Nehemath

He was not born a villain. He and Touchfire grew up with the same dream: to become the greatest wizards on NeoVarion. They trained together, pushed each other, and were close in the way brothers often are.

Then, during an expedition into the wilds of NeoVarion, a Shadowfang snake bit Nehemath. The venom did not kill him. It found the darkest corner of his heart and fed it. Ambition became obsession. Pride became cruelty. The brother Touchfire knew disappeared year by year, until nothing was left but the hunger.

He took over the Forest of Haag, cursed the Dwellers into aggression, and ruled from the shadows. He was the most powerful wizard on NeoVarion. He was also completely alone.

Origin
Brother of Touchfire, corrupted by a Shadowfang snake bite
Goal
To be the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. To dominate. To never be second to anyone, especially not his brother.
Downfall
Defeated by Touchfire in the Forest of Haag. His powers absorbed. His curse broken. The Dwellers breathed again.

The Story

The Journey

Prologue
The Mission Begins
The king of Slinsil learns that Krill is planning an attack. He assembles five spies and sends them into the heart of enemy territory to steal the battle plans hidden in the dungeon above Kraznir's castle.
Day 1
Escaping the Dungeon
The spies find the battle plans and important documents inside the dungeon of Kraznir. With guards on all sides, Max turns into a GlassWalker to move unseen. But a careless moment wakes the sleeping guards and forces the team to run for their lives through a hidden tunnel.
Day 2
Ambush in the Mountains
On the mountain path home, three giant spiders rise from a hole in the ground and launch a brutal attack. Only Littlejohn, immune to their venom, can survive their bites. He takes five and still gets back up. The team destroys two spiders together. Max finishes the last one with brute alien force.
Day 3
The River Glin
A massive river filled with piranhas stands between the spies and home. Their boat holds only four. They cross in shifts, losing a bag of food along the way. When they return for Sarason, they find her barely alive, covered in claw marks. The chest is gone. But she saved the battle plans.
Day 4
The Stony Desert
A colossal three-headed winged monster called the Margatroth hunts them across the desert. Nearly blind but enormous, it can only be killed by cutting off all three heads. The team works together in a desperate, chaotic battle. Touchfire ends it with the biggest fireball he has ever created.
Day 5
The Forest of Haag
The Forest of Haag is ruled by Nehemath, Touchfire's own brother, corrupted by a Shadowfang snake years ago. He has cursed the Dwellers into seeing every outsider as a threat. The two brothers fight. Touchfire wins and absorbs Nehemath's powers, becoming the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. He does not celebrate.
The Final Battle
Slinsil
Krill reaches Slinsil before them. He kills the spies one by one. Max learns the truth: the battle plans contain the coordinates to collapse NeoVarion's core. The mission was never just a war. It was the end of the world. Max uses the Arcwatch's last power to revive his friends, giving his own life in exchange.
Epilogue
One Year Later
Touchfire lives in the Forest of Haag with the Dwellers. Sarason becomes the new general of Slinsil. Littlejohn finds emeralds on his first day of mining. Calypso collects the rarest stones in the world from the River Glin. Four spies, four new lives. All of them missing the one who made it possible.
The Map

Places of NeoVarion

🏰
Slinsil
The heart of NeoVarion A kingdom built unknowingly above the planet's core. Beautiful and unaware of the cosmic target painted on it. Home to the king, the army, and a street kid named Max.
⛓️
The Dungeon of Kraznir
A massive dungeon above the castle of Krill. Where the battle plans were hidden. Where the mission truly began. Full of sleeping guards, locked chests, and a tunnel with a very large hole at the end.
⛰️
The Mountain Path
The rocky road between Kraznir and Slinsil. Hundreds of kilometers of stone and altitude. Home to the giant spiders. The place where Littlejohn proved that being immune to venom is its own kind of superpower.
🌊
The River Glin
A massive river filled with piranhas that separates the mountain path from the desert. Dangerous to cross. Beautiful to look at. Home to the strongest and rarest stones in all of NeoVarion, something Calypso discovers much later.
🪨
The Stony Desert
A vast, empty wasteland of rock and heat. The hunting ground of the Margatroth, a colossal three-headed winged monster that is nearly blind but barely needs to see. The place where Touchfire created the biggest fireball of his life.
🌲
The Forest of Haag
Ancient and alive, built tree by tree by the Dwellers as a monument to peace. Corrupted by Nehemath into something dark and aggressive. Restored when the curse broke. Now home to Touchfire, the Dwellers, and a new beginning.
