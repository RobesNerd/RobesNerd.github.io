Max Strange

A street kid chosen by a dying star to carry the weight of a world he never knew was his to save.

Origin

Max grew up on the streets of Slinsil with no family, no home, and no future. He learned to survive by being fast, smart, and invisible. He never thought of himself as a hero. He never had the chance to.

Everything changed the night Magnulos crashed on NeoVarion. The alien had crossed the universe wounded and dying, hunted by Krill, carrying one last mission. He found Max in the dark streets of Slinsil and fused the Arcwatch to his wrist before closing his eyes for the last time.

The Arcwatch

The Arcwatch is not just a weapon. It is a piece of alien technology that bonds to a single host and grants the ability to transform into different alien forms, each with a unique power. It was once used by Magnulos to defeat Vael, Krill's father.

The transformations are not always fully in Max's control. Under extreme stress, the watch can switch forms unexpectedly. This is both his greatest strength and his most dangerous weakness.

Timeline

Before the Mission

Raised alone on the streets of Slinsil after losing his parents. Learned to survive by instinct. Never imagined a life beyond the city walls.

The Night of the Crash

Magnulos finds Max and fuses the Arcwatch to his wrist. Max learns that a cosmic conqueror is coming and that he is now the only thing standing in the way.

The Mission

Chosen by the king to lead the five spies into Kraznir. Over five days and five near-deaths, Max discovers what he is capable of and what he is willing to lose.

The Final Battle

Krill kills the four spies one by one. Max learns the truth about NeoVarion's core. He activates Revenant, gives his life, and brings his friends back. No regrets.

The Epilogue

Max is gone. But four people are alive because of him. The four spies miss a leader, a father, a friend. His absence is the loudest thing in every room.
