Magnulos

He crossed the universe wounded and dying to find someone who could stop what was coming. He found a street kid. He hoped that would be enough.

The Last Survivor

Magnulos and Krill were the only survivors when their shared home planet was destroyed. But where Krill inherited his father's mission of destruction, Magnulos spent his life doing the opposite: protecting worlds from the kind of end he had already lived through.

He once used the Arcwatch to defeat Vael, Krill's father, and stop the destruction of a second planet. He spared Vael's life. It was a mistake that would haunt the universe for years to come.

The Final Journey

When Krill finally caught up to him and their home planet was destroyed, Magnulos used the last of his strength to escape to NeoVarion. Wounded, hunted, and dying, he had one goal left: find someone who could carry the Arcwatch and stop Krill before he destroyed another world.

He found Max on the streets of Slinsil. A street kid with no family, no future, and no idea what was coming. Magnulos fused the Arcwatch to Max's wrist, passed him everything he needed to know, and closed his eyes for the last time.

He gave me this watch and said one thing before he closed his eyes. Stop him.
Max Strange, on the night Magnulos died

Magnulos never saw the outcome. He never knew if Max would succeed, if Krill would fall, if NeoVarion would survive. He made a choice with the last strength he had and trusted a stranger with the fate of a planet.

He was right to trust him.
