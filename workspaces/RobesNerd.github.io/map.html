Day 1
⛓️
The Dungeon of Kraznir
Enemy Territory
A massive dungeon built above the castle of Krill, king of Kraznir. Inside its walls, the five spies found the battle plans, which they later discover are really the coordinates to collapse NeoVarion's core.
What Happened
The spies retrieved the plans but woke the guards escaping. Max turned invisible to move unseen. Touchfire blocked the tunnel with a stone wall. They ran into the dark with everything they came for.
Day 2
⛰️
The Mountain Path
Danger Zone
Hundreds of kilometers of rock, altitude, and silence. The road between Kraznir and Slinsil runs through these mountains. Beautiful from a distance. Terrifying up close.
What Happened
Three giant spiders rose from a hole in the ground and ambushed the group. Littlejohn took five bites and survived. Max used a powerful alien form to finish the last two. No one expected Littlejohn to be the hero that day.
Day 3
🌊
The River Glin
Natural Barrier
A massive river filled with piranhas. Its waters are dangerous, its crossing is complicated, and somewhere beneath its surface lie the strongest and rarest stones in all of NeoVarion.
What Happened
The team crossed in shifts using a small boat. Sarason stayed behind to guard the chest. When they returned, she was barely alive, covered in claw marks. The chest was gone. The battle plans were in her hands.
Day 4
🪨
The Stony Desert
Wasteland
A vast, empty expanse of stone and heat. Almost nothing lives here. Almost. The Margatroth, a colossal three-headed winged monster, calls this desert its hunting ground.
What Happened
The Margatroth hunted the group across the desert. Nearly blind but enormous, it needed all three heads removed to die. Touchfire ended the fight with the biggest fireball he had ever created. The ground shook when the monster fell.
Day 5
🌲
The Forest of Haag
Ancient and Alive
Built tree by tree by the Dwellers as a monument to peace. Corrupted by Nehemath into a dark fortress. When the curse broke, the forest began to breathe again. It is now home to Touchfire and the Dwellers.
What Happened
Nehemath, Touchfire's own brother, ruled the forest under his curse. The two brothers fought. Touchfire won and absorbed his powers, becoming the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. He stood very still while the others cheered.
Home Kingdom
🏰
Slinsil
Heart of NeoVarion
The kingdom of Slinsil sits unknowingly above the core of NeoVarion itself. To destroy Slinsil is to destroy the entire planet. Only Krill knew this from the start. Max discovered it at the final battle.
What Happened
Krill arrived before the spies. He killed them one by one. Max learned the truth, used the Arcwatch's last power, gave his life to revive his friends, and defeated Krill. NeoVarion survived. Max did not. He had no regrets.
