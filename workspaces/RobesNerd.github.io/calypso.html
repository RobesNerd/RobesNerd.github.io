Elf, Scout of Veyln

Calypso

Exiled by her own people. She joined the mission because she had nowhere else to go. She stayed because she finally found somewhere to belong.

The Elf

Calypso can see in total darkness and move almost completely unseen. In the dungeon of Kraznir, those abilities are the difference between the mission succeeding and the whole team dying in the dark. She is not the loudest spy in the group. She is often the most essential.

She also has an instinct for reading people and situations that the others lack. She notices things before they become problems. She just rarely says anything until it matters.

The Exile

She comes from Veyln, a hidden elven city built deep underground, lit by glowing crystals instead of sunlight. Her people are secretive and isolationist. They do not leave, and they do not welcome outsiders.

Calypso was exiled. She rarely speaks about why. What she will say is that the king of Slinsil offered her a mission and, with it, a place to belong. She took it without hesitation. That was the first time in a long time that anyone had asked her to be part of something.

Veyln, The Underground City

Built deep beneath the surface of NeoVarion, Veyln is a city of silence and crystal light. Its people have lived underground for so long that they see in the dark as naturally as breathing. They speak in low voices and keep their world hidden from everyone above.

Calypso carries Veyln with her everywhere, in the way she moves, the way she watches, the way she waits before speaking. But she has no intention of going back.

I do not need many words. I just need to be in the right place at the right moment.
Calypso

Timeline

Life in Veyln

Grows up in the underground city, surrounded by crystal light and silence. Learns to move without sound and see without light. Feels, quietly, that she does not belong there.

The Exile

Exiled from Veyln. She does not speak much about what happened. She carries it with her in the way she holds herself, careful and self-contained, as if trying to take up as little space as possible.

The Mission

Joins the five spies. Her abilities in the dark make the dungeon escape possible. She watches the group carefully, says very little, and understands everything.

After NeoVarion

Goes to the River Glin to collect its rare stones. No one knows exactly why. Maybe she just needed somewhere quiet. Maybe she found something there that felt like home.
