Wizard, Greatest of NeoVarion

Touchfire

He dreamed the same dream as his brother. He just chose a different path to reach it, and that difference destroyed everything.

The Wizard

Touchfire is one of the most powerful wizards on NeoVarion. He can create and control fire with precision, turn enemies to stone, and after defeating his brother, he became the single greatest wielder of magic the world has ever seen.

His spells, before absorbing Nehemath's power, lasted only five minutes. That limitation forced him to be creative and strategic in every fight. It made him dangerous in a way raw power alone never could.

The Man Behind the Flame

Beneath the power is a person carrying a grief most people will never understand. He and Nehemath grew up with the same dream. They trained together, pushed each other, and were close in a way brothers often are.

When the Shadowfang snake bit Nehemath and changed him, Touchfire lost his brother years before he ever had to fight him. The fight in the Forest of Haag was not a victory. It was the end of something that had been dying for a long time.

He was my brother before he was my enemy. I never forgot that. Not even at the end.
Touchfire

Touchfire and Nehemath, The Brothers

They grew up together with one shared dream: to become the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. They were inseparable, competitive, and genuinely close. Then a Shadowfang snake bit Nehemath during an expedition, slowly corrupting his heart and turning ambition into obsession.

The two brothers tried to stay together for years after the bite. But Nehemath grew colder, more controlling, more cruel. Eventually they made a vow to destroy each other, not entirely out of hate, but out of the grief of losing what they once had.

When Touchfire defeats Nehemath in the Forest of Haag and absorbs his powers, the others cheer. Touchfire stands still for a long moment. He is the greatest wizard on NeoVarion now. It feels like nothing.

Timeline

Childhood

Grows up alongside his brother Nehemath, sharing the dream of becoming the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. They train together, push each other, and are genuinely close.

The Shadowfang Bite

During an early expedition, a Shadowfang snake bites Nehemath. The change is slow but permanent. Touchfire watches his brother drift into darkness year by year, powerless to stop it.

The Vow

The two brothers, now enemies, vow to destroy each other. It is the most painful thing either of them has ever done.

The Mission

Joins the five spies. Proves essential in nearly every battle, from the spider ambush to the Margatroth in the desert.

The Forest of Haag

Fights and defeats Nehemath. Absorbs his powers. Becomes the greatest wizard on NeoVarion. Stands very still while everyone else celebrates.

After NeoVarion
