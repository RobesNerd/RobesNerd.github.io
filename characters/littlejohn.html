Littlejohn

He joined for the gold and told himself that was enough. He was wrong. That might be the best thing that ever happened to him.

The Dwarf

Littlejohn complains constantly, tires easily, and is exactly as useless as he looks in a straight fight. He is also the only member of the group who is completely immune to venom, which turns out to matter quite a lot when three giant spiders decide to bite him five times in a row.

He is not the strongest or the fastest. But he gets back up every single time. That is not nothing. In fact, on this mission, that is everything.

The Real Littlejohn

He was kicked out of Clan Durnhold for being considered too weak by dwarven standards. It was the most humiliating moment of his life. He joined the mission purely for the gold, telling himself that friendship was for people who could afford it.

His complaints are real. But underneath them is a dwarf who is terrified of being seen as useless again. Every time he gets back up, every time he pushes through the mountain and the desert and the spider bites, he is quietly proving something to himself.

I am not complaining. I am just saying it is very far and my legs are very short.
Littlejohn, somewhere in the mountains

Timeline

Clan Durnhold

Grows up among dwarves who measure worth in strength and speed. He is neither strong enough nor fast enough by their standards. The day they kick him out is the most humiliating of his life.

Joining the Mission

Signs up purely for the gold. Tells himself he does not need anyone. Starts complaining almost immediately.

The Spider Ambush

Takes five spider bites and survives. His immunity to venom is the only reason the team wins. He wakes up to find his friends still there, waiting. That matters more than the gold.

The River, The Desert, The Forest

Keeps going. Every single time. Short legs and all.

After NeoVarion

Becomes a miner. On his very first day, he finds more than ten emeralds. He gets a bit rich. He earns it.
